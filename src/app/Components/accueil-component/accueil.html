About our project

Our project is based on data of an API. Which one ? We use data of forces and crimes in United Kingdom. This probably doesn't mean anything to you, but you will see by surfing on our app that there is a part for all of forces in UK and some information about it. There is also a part for all crimes that happened in UK this year and which police force handled it.

There is a map...

There is a surprise in this project, a map ! In fact, in the last part of the app, you will see a map of the United Kingdom which is composed of the differents police forces. The color of each zone depends on the number of crimes. Light yellow means a secure zone and a dark red means that there are lots of crimes in this zone.
